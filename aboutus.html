About Us

"Spread The Smile" is a donation platform that allows you to support a cause of your choice from about across NGOs that have been scrutinised for transparency & credibility. We do not raise funds for ourselves. We help you donate to these NGOs..
We tell you exactly where your donations went. We ensure that most of your contribution reaches the organization you support except for when your donations are more than the need of the NGO. In that case the remaining amount go to the Warehouse.
